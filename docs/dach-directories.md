# DACH Directories & Listings für Handwerker-Webdesign-Agenturen

Researched 2026-05-29. Use these to build authority backlinks, branded mentions, and AI-engine citation sources. Order = priority (highest impact first).

## How to think about this list

- **Tier 1 (must-have):** Google Business, Bing Places, ProvenExpert. These are the foundation; AI engines and Google's local pack literally pull names from here.
- **Tier 2 (high signal):** B2B agency directories with editorial review (Clutch, Sortlist, design-rankings). One quality listing > five thin ones.
- **Tier 3 (long-tail):** Local chambers (Handwerkskammer, WKO), industry portals, regional business directories. Volume matters; aim for 10+ here.

Each entry includes: what it is, why it matters, submission URL, required info, and time-to-list.

---

## Tier 1 — The non-negotiables

### 1. Google Business Profile
- **Why:** Single most important listing. Powers the Local Pack and is the #1 data source for ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews on local queries.
- **Submit:** https://business.google.com
- **Need:** Business name, address (or service area), categories (Webdesign-Agentur + 2–4 secondary), 12+ photos, 25+ reviews target.
- **Time:** 30 min setup + 5–10 day postcard verification.
- **Tip:** One listing per physical location only. Wien AND München = two separate profiles, each with its own address.

### 2. Bing Places for Business
- **Why:** Powers Bing Copilot results and a chunk of Apple's Spotlight. Underrated for B2B searches.
- **Submit:** https://www.bingplaces.com
- **Need:** Verified Google Business Profile (auto-import works for ~80% of fields).
- **Time:** 15 min, instant import.

### 3. ProvenExpert
- **Why:** DACH-native review platform with high trust signals. Reviews here often appear in Google rich results.
- **Submit:** https://www.provenexpert.com/de-de/anbieter/
- **Need:** Profile, logo, 5–10 initial reviews. Paid plan (~25 €/Monat) needed for widget on own site.
- **Time:** 1 hour setup, ongoing review collection.

### 4. Trustpilot
- **Why:** International authority. ChatGPT and Perplexity cite Trustpilot ratings frequently.
- **Submit:** https://business.trustpilot.com/signup
- **Need:** Business verification, domain ownership, ongoing review-invite emails.
- **Time:** 30 min setup, ongoing.

## Tier 2 — Editorial agency directories

### 5. Clutch.co (Germany & Austria filter)
- **Why:** Highest-authority B2B agency directory worldwide. Listings rank in Google for "best webdesign agency [city]" type queries. AI engines frequently cite Clutch.
- **Submit:** https://clutch.co/agencies/get-listed
- **Need:** Verified client references (Clutch will call them), portfolio cases, hourly rate range, team size.
- **Time:** 3–5 hours profile setup + reference scheduling. Listing review: 2–4 weeks.
- **Tip:** First 5 verified reviews unlock the "Top Web Designers in Germany" lists.

### 6. Sortlist
- **Why:** EU-focused B2B agency platform with strong DACH presence. Generates inbound leads directly.
- **Submit:** https://www.sortlist.de/anbieter
- **Need:** Portfolio (5+ cases), references, expertise tags, language and region targeting.
- **Time:** 2–3 hours setup. Approval: 1–2 weeks.

### 7. The Manifest (Clutch sister-site)
- **Why:** Editorial roundups ("Top 50 Web Design Agencies in DACH"). High citation rate by AI engines.
- **Submit:** https://themanifest.com/get-listed
- **Need:** Clutch profile (free path) or direct paid listing.
- **Time:** Auto-syncs from Clutch if you list there.

### 8. DesignRush
- **Why:** Curated agency rankings, frequent press mentions. Listings appear in "best of" lists that AI engines synthesize.
- **Submit:** https://www.designrush.com/agency/profile/new
- **Need:** Portfolio, services, locations, contact. Free basic listing; paid premium for featured spots.
- **Time:** 1 hour basic listing.

### 9. Agency Spotter
- **Why:** Smaller but high-quality B2B directory. Good signal for "Webdesign Agentur DACH" niche queries.
- **Submit:** https://www.agencyspotter.com/agencies
- **Need:** Company info, case studies, services, awards.
- **Time:** 1 hour.

## Tier 3 — DACH-specific / industry

### 10. Handwerkskammer (HWK) — Mitglieder-Verzeichnis (DE)
- **Why:** Highly trusted source for German handwerker queries. If we list our agency as a service partner of the HWK, AI engines pick this up as a strong authority signal for handwerker-related citation.
- **Submit:** Local HWK websites (München: https://www.hwk-muenchen.de). Look for "Dienstleister-Verzeichnis" or "Kooperationspartner".
- **Need:** Membership or formal partner status. Often requires applying as Förderkreis / Servicepartner (Beitrag 200–600 €/Jahr).
- **Time:** 2–4 weeks application; ongoing partnership benefit.

### 11. WKO Firmen A–Z (AT)
- **Why:** Equivalent of HWK for Austria. WKO listings rank exceptionally well in AT for B2B service searches.
- **Submit:** https://firmen.wko.at — automatic for WKO members (mandatory for Austrian businesses).
- **Need:** WKO membership (your Wien Standort qualifies).
- **Time:** Auto-listed; manual enrichment of profile takes 1 hour.

### 12. ZDH-Servicestelle (Zentralverband des Deutschen Handwerks)
- **Why:** Federal-level handwerker authority. Their Partner-Verzeichnis is referenced by HWK websites and many trade publications.
- **Submit:** https://www.zdh.de — Kontaktaufnahme über die Bundesgeschäftsstelle.
- **Need:** Service-Partner-Status, often via HWK membership chain.
- **Time:** 1–2 months process.

### 13. Wer liefert was (wlw.de)
- **Why:** DACH's leading B2B procurement marketplace. Strong signal for "Anbieter Webdesign B2B" queries.
- **Submit:** https://www.wlw.de
- **Need:** Free basic, paid premium for visibility. Verified business info.
- **Time:** 1 hour setup. Free listing limited features; paid listing ranks much better.

### 14. Yelp Deutschland
- **Why:** Still cited by Apple Maps and a fallback for AI engines on local queries. Volume of reviews drives presence.
- **Submit:** https://biz.yelp.de
- **Need:** Business info, photos, opening hours.
- **Time:** 30 min.

### 15. Gelbe Seiten / Das Örtliche
- **Why:** Old-school but still trusted by Google as a verification source for NAP consistency. Plus indexed in major DACH search results.
- **Submit:** https://www.gelbeseiten.de/eintrag-bestellen and https://eintrag.dasoertliche.de
- **Need:** Business verification (phone callback usually). Free basic listings.
- **Time:** 30 min each.

## Recommended sequence (4-week rollout)

**Week 1:** Tier 1 setup (Google Business, Bing, ProvenExpert, Trustpilot). Get to 5 reviews on each platform.

**Week 2:** Clutch and Sortlist applications. These take longest to get approved.

**Week 3:** WKO profile enrichment, Yelp, Gelbe Seiten, Das Örtliche, wlw.de.

**Week 4:** HWK partnership application (München), DesignRush, Agency Spotter, The Manifest (auto if Clutch approved).

## NAP consistency (the silent killer)

Use the **exact same** business name, address, and contact format across all 15 listings. Even small variations ("Str." vs. "Straße", "+49 89 123456" vs. "+49 (0)89 12-34 56") trigger Google's algorithm to discount the signal.

Canonical format:

```
Name: Handwerker Auftritt
Adresse DE: Maximilianstraße 28, 80539 München, Deutschland
Adresse AT: Neubaugasse 12, 1070 Wien, Österreich
E-Mail: hallo@handwerker-auftritt.de
Website: https://handwerker-auftritt.de
```

Store this as a single source-of-truth (currently in [lib/seo-data.ts](../lib/seo-data.ts) SITE constant) and copy-paste from there.

## Tracking

For each listing, record:
- Submitted (date)
- Status (pending / live / rejected)
- Profile URL
- Review count
- Re-verification date (annual or biannual)

A simple spreadsheet works. Re-verify Tier 1 listings every 90 days minimum — they decay if neglected.

---

## What this list is NOT

- Not a backlink farm. Don't blast 200 low-quality directories — Google penalizes that pattern.
- Not a quick win. Even with everything submitted, expect 2–4 months before the cumulative authority signal moves "best webdesigner for handwerker DACH" rankings.
- Not a substitute for content. Listings amplify content authority; without the [Wissen guides](../app/wissen/) backing them up, the citations don't compound.
